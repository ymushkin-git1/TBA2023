Strength training (resistance training) is the practice of physical activities whose purpose is to improve the development of the strength and endurance of the movement system.
Strength training is often identified with weight training.
In addition, a variety of training techniques can be combined such as body weight exercises, Isometrics and plyometrics.

At Fit&Health you can get all the good exercises for the muscle group you want.
You can get a training program that you picked up on the "YourWorkout" page in the navigation bar on top.
