Peaky Blinders

A Crime Drama Set in Birmingham, England

Following the Shelby family, who lead the Peaky Blinders gang, the series begins in 1919, just after the end of World War I, and covers the events of the next several years.

The Peaky Blinders, named for their practice of sewing razor blades into the peaks of their caps, control much of the city's criminal underworld, including illegal betting, loan sharking, and black market goods. The gang is led by Thomas Shelby, a cunning and ruthless man who will stop at nothing to protect his family and maintain their power.

As the series progresses, the Peaky Blinders find themselves under threat from rival gangs, the police, and the government, who are all determined to bring them down. Meanwhile, Thomas struggles with his own inner demons, as he tries to balance his loyalty to his family with his own personal ambitions.

The show features an ensemble cast of characters, each with their own motivations and personal struggles, creating a complex and dramatic story that keeps audiences on the edge of their seats.
