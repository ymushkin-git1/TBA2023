Whole-wheat bun:
The whole-wheat hamburger bun has a lot of benefits for your body.
 - It boosts your health and provides the nutrients your body needs to function at its best.

- It is a source of essential minerals like iron, zinc, and potassium.
- Although the bun is low on carbs, it is a great source of energy (the bun is filled with carbohydrates that your body breaks down into glucose, which is the main source of energy for your body, especially your brain.)
- It may prevent nutrient deficiencies
- It may help with appetite control
- It is Good for the gut
- It fits most diet plans
