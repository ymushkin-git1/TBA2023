General Q&A

this page is a simple one, your are gonna find answeres for common yet important questions about fitness.

How much should I exercise?

Like many questions, the answer depends on your goals, however the best tip I can give you is to listen to your body. If you feel like
you need to rest, rest. For starters however I recommed to start exercising 3 times a week.

How much should I rest between workouts?

For starters I recommend resting around 1 to 2 days between every workout.

How much should I eat?

Like I showed you in the Calorie calculator page, It also depend's on your goals, if you want to lose bodyfat, burn more than you consume,
if you want to fain muscle and fat consume more than you burn.

Missing a couple of workouts is ok and wouldn't be too bad. However after around 3 weeks of not exercising you will start losing muscle mass.
