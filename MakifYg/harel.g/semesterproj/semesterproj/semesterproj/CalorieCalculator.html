Daily Calories Burned Calculator

This calculator is a tool for those of you who want to gain or lose weight. The rules are simple,
you burn more than you consume, you lose weight,
you consume more than you burn, you gain weight.
I would like to note that both being overweight and underweight ARE VERY UNHEALTHY. talk to a doctor or a proffesional about your nutrition and fitness goals.
This Page is a tool, use it wisely.
