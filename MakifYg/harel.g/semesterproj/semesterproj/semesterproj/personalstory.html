My Personal Story

My fitness journey started around two months after my 14th birthday. I used to be really skinny, not athletic and physically weak.
I was insecure about these things and I wanted to change them, so I signed up for a gym membership. I had a few friends join with me.
We got our own training programs and we started going to the gym regularly. We were going three times a week and it has become a habbit very quickly. Around half a year into the journey we started seeing results, people startet complimenting us telling us that we got bigger. It was nice to hear
but to be honest it wasn't what was keeping us going, it was the fun we had. We loved experementing with different excersises and seeing what we like
the most, we loved getting stronger and adding weight to the machines, we loved chatting with people in the gym and getting to know new people and eating healthier.

It has been aroud 20 months since I joined the gym And I can confidentally say that it was one of my best decisions.
It gave me expriences that I would never forget, relationships with peeople I would have never met otherwise and the confidence to talk to them.
I built this website to inspire people to incoroperate fitness into their lives and to show them it is very simple.
