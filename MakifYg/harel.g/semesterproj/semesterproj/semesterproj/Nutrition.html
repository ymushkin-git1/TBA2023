Nutrition

For many people the hardest part about fitness isn't the exercises. It is nutrition.
Nutrition is just as, if not more important as exsercise when it comes to fitness. People often get really confused when they start
researching about food and they get demotivated. This is where this page comes in! In this page you can find some really nutritious foods that won't break the bank.

nutrientnutrient sourcesNutrient purpose
proteineggs, all types of meat/chicken/fish, dairy products, legumes and nuts Every cell in the human body contains protein. The basic structure of protein is a chain of amino acids. You need protein in your diet to help your body repair cells and make new ones. Protein is also important for growth and development in children, teens, and pregnant women.
vitamins&mineralsall fruits and vegetables What Do Vitamins and Minerals Do? Vitamins and minerals boost the immune system, support normal growth and development, and help cells and organs do their jobs.
carbohydrateswhole grains, sweet potatoe, oatsCarbohydrates provide the body with glucose, which is converted to energy used to support bodily functions and physical activity.
fatsnuts, avocados, fish, Dietary fats are essential to give your body energy and to support cell function. They also help protect your organs and help keep your body warm. Fats help your body absorb some nutrients and produce important hormones, too.
waterwaterWater helps your body: Keep a normal temperature. Lubricate and cushion joints. Protect your spinal cord and other sensitive tissues. Get rid of wastes through urination, perspiration, and bowel movements.
