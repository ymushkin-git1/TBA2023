Neymar JR


Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar,
is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for:
Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team.
A prolific goalscorer and renowned playmaker, he is regarded as one of the best players in the world.
Neymar has scored at least 100 goals for three different clubs, making him one of three players to achieve this.

Nicknames: Ney, Menino, Samba Lannister.
