The BMW i3 is an all-electric compact hatchback first released in 2014, part of the BMW i series of electric vehicles. It is powered by a lithium-ion battery pack and electric motor producing 170 horsepower and has a range of up to 153 miles per charge. It can be charged using a Level 2 or Level 3 charger, and takes around 40 minutes to charge to 80%. Its unique design features a carbon-fiber reinforced plastic passenger cell, aluminum chassis and recycled plastic exterior body panels. Standard features include a 6.5-inch infotainment display, automatic climate control and rearview camera, with optional upgrades such as a larger infotainment display, premium sound system and sunroof. It also includes advanced safety features such as parking sensors, lane departure warning, automatic emergency braking and forward-collision warning system. BMW also offers a range extender version of the i3 which uses a small gasoline engine to generate power and extend its range.
