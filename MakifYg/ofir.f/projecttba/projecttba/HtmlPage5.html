Panamera 4 E-Hybrid

The Porsche Panamera 4 E-Hybrid is a plug-in hybrid luxury sedan from Porsche. It was first introduced in 2018 and is based on the second-generation Panamera. The Panamera 4 E-Hybrid is powered by a 2.9-liter V6 engine and an electric motor for a combined output of 462 horsepower. It is capable of 0-60 mph in 4.4 seconds and has a top speed of 172 mph. It has a range of up to 31 miles on electric power alone and can be recharged from a standard household outlet. It also comes with a host of driver aids and safety features. The Panamera 4 E-Hybrid is an ideal choice for those looking for a luxury sedan with the performance and efficiency of a plug-in hybrid.

TAYCAN

The Porsche Taycan is an all-electric, four-door sports sedan from Porsche. It was unveiled in September 2019 and is the first fully electric vehicle from the German automaker. The Taycan is powered by two electric motors, one on each axle, for all-wheel drive. It has a range of up to 300 miles and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds. It also features advanced driver assistance systems and an interior with a large infotainment screen. The Taycan is available in several trim levels, including the Turbo and Turbo S. Prices start at around $103,000.

Porsche Mission E Cross Turismo

The Porsche Mission E Cross Turismo is a high-performance all-electric four-door crossover concept car, unveiled by Porsche in March 2018. It features a longer wheelbase than the Mission E concept car, with a more rugged design. It is powered by two electric motors, with a combined output of 600 horsepower and a 0-60 mph time of less than 3.5 seconds. It has a range of 310 miles, and supports both DC fast charging and inductive charging. The interior features a large touchscreen display, four individual seats, and a spacious luggage compartment.

Porsche Cayenne E-Hybrid

The Porsche Cayenne E-Hybrid redefines what an SUV can be. It’s powerful. Capable. Luxurious. Most importantly, though, it’s efficient. Its turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine, combined with a hybrid electric power system, produces the performance you’d expect from a Porsche with efficiency you’ve likely never experienced in an SUV before. A 17.9 kWh lithium-ion battery pack provides up to 34 miles of electric-only range without sacrificing the incredible performance available from a combined 455 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. Of course, the technology doesn’t stop there – a customizable heads-up display provides key info to the driver without drawing attention away from the road ahead.
