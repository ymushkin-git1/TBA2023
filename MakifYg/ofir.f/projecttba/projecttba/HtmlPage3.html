X

The Tesla Model X is an all-electric luxury crossover SUV produced by Tesla, Inc. It was first introduced in 2015, and it is still in production as of 2021. It features a unique falcon-wing door design for the rear doors, allowing for easy access to the second and third rows of seating. The Model X is available in several versions, the Long Range, Performance and Plaid. The Long Range version has a range of 371 miles, the Performance version has a range of 371 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 2.5 seconds and the Plaid version has a range of 390 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 1.99 seconds. All versions have all-wheel drive, and feature a 17-inch touchscreen in the dashboard, providing access to the car's controls and entertainment system. Additionally, the Model X is equipped with advanced driver assistance systems, including autopilot, which provides semi-autonomous driving capabilities.

Y

The Tesla Model Y is an all-electric compact SUV produced by Tesla, Inc. It was first introduced in March 2019 and it is still in production as of 2021. The Model Y is available in several versions, including the Long Range, Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive, and Performance. The Long Range version has a range of 326 miles, the Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive version has a range of 326 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 4.8 seconds and the Performance version has a range of 315 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 3.5 seconds. All versions have a 15-inch touchscreen in the dashboard, providing access to the car's controls and entertainment system, and are equipped with advanced driver assistance systems, including autopilot, which provides semi-autonomous driving capabilities. The Model Y also features a spacious interior and a panoramic glass roof. It is designed to seat up to 7 adults and it has a front trunk (frunk) and a rear trunk, providing ample storage space.



S

The Tesla Model S is a luxury electric sedan produced by Tesla, Inc. It was first introduced in 2012 and it is still in production as of 2021. The Model S is available in several versions, including the Long Range, Plaid, and Plaid+. The Long Range version has a range of 373 miles, the Plaid version has a range of 390 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 1.99 seconds and the Plaid+ version has a range of 390 miles and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 1.99 seconds. The Model S features a sleek design, with a 17-inch touchscreen in the dashboard, providing access to the car's controls and entertainment system. Additionally, the Model S is equipped with advanced driver assistance systems, including autopilot, which provides semi-autonomous driving capabilities. The Model S also has a spacious interior, it's designed to seat up to five adults and it has a front trunk (frunk) providing ample storage space. The vehicle also features a panoramic glass roof, allowing for an open and airy cabin.


3

The Tesla Model 3 is a compact luxury electric sedan produced by Tesla, Inc. It was first introduced in 2017 and is still in production as of 2021. It is available in several versions, including Standard Range, Standard Range Plus, Long Range, Performance and Performance Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive, with ranges varying from 263 to 358 miles and acceleration from 0-60 mph ranging from 5.3 seconds to 3.2 seconds. It features a sleek and minimalist design, with a 15-inch touchscreen in the dashboard, advanced driver assistance systems including autopilot, seating for up to five adults, and a front trunk (frunk) for storage. It is Tesla's most affordable vehicle, with a starting price of around $35,000.

CYBERTRUCK

The Tesla Cybertruck is an all-electric pickup truck produced by Tesla, Inc. It was first unveiled in November 2019 but is not yet in production as of 2021. It is available in several versions, including Single Motor Rear-Wheel Drive, Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive, and Tri-Motor All-Wheel Drive, with ranges varying from 250 miles to 500 miles and acceleration from 0-60 mph ranging from 6.5 seconds to 2.9 seconds. It features a futuristic, angular design made of ultra-hard 30X cold-rolled stainless-steel structural skin, spacious interior that seats up to six adults, a front trunk (frunk) and a rear trunk for storage, advanced driver assistance systems, and a 17-inch touchscreen in the dashboard. The availability and information provided are subject to change.
